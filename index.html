Albert Einstein

A tribute page to an amazing mind

Einstein in 1947, two years after the end of WWII

Bio

  • Born: March 14, 1879 (Ulm, Germany)
  • Died: April 18, 1955 (New Jersey, USA)
  • Education: PhD, University of Zurich (1905)
  • Most Famous For: General/Special Relativity

Major Events

  • 1905: Publishes matter-energy equivalence (E=mc2)
  • 1922: Wins Nobel prize for Physics
  • 1939: Letter to FDR which led to Manhattan Project
